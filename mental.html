Mental Illness

Mental illnesses are health conditions involving changes in emotion, thinking or behavior (or a combination of these). Mental illnesses can be associated with distress and/or problems functioning in social, work or family activities. Mental illness is nothing to be ashamed of.

Answer the following question to know which Mental illness you might have

Do you often have a feeling of nervousness, restlessness or tense.

Do you often have a feelings of sadness, tearfulness, emptiness or hopelessness.

Do you often see or hear things that don't really exist

Do you often have low self-esteem or exesive guilt

Do you often have a feeling of carelessness and lack of attention to detail
